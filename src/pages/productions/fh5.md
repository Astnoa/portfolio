During the development of _Forza Horizon 5_ I was part of the terrain team and responsible for both procedural and manual terrain authoring. As well as decoration and supporting the other environment teams.
Many thanks to Ole Groenbaek for providing feedback and guiding my work process during the production. Also credits to the rest of the terrain and environment team.

#### Gran Caldera Volcano
One of the areas I was privileged to work on was the Gran Caldera Volcano, which is where the game starts.
