European Truck Racing Championship is a FIA licensed simcade truck racing game. During it’s development I was the main level artist. I did the preliminary visual and gameplay setup for each level in the game. Making sure it matches with provided plans and reference.  
After which I also did a large portion of the decoration. Some levels I worked more on than others. While working on the levels I also partly managed interns and junior artist to assist in asset creation and decoration tasks.
Furthermore, I also did a variety of asset creation, authoring and some work on lighting. And lastly, I also made some screenshots that were used for marketing.

Credits to everyone in the art team.

### Hungary Speedway

The Hungary circuit is the only fictional race track in the game which was completely created from scratch. After the producer gave me a rough sketch of the track layout I was given a lot of creative freedom to design and decorate the rest of the circuit.
